Why Choose Us ?

Mathias Catholic Hospital was established in 1991 by the JMJ Sisters in a small structure through the instrumentality of the late Bishop of then Sunyani Diocese, Most Rev. James Kojo Owusu. Since then the JMJ Sisters have continued to manage the hospital. It is the only hospital in the Pru East District and serves as a major referral point for about 10 public and private health institutions within the surroundings districts The hospital has a bed capacity of 205 being handled by staff strength of 401 currently. The hospital is under the political administration of the Pru East District Assembly of the Bono East Region of Ghana

OUR VISION

To provide quality health care service in the most effective, efficient and innovative manner, specific to the needs of the communities we serve and at all times acknowledging the dignity of the Patients.

OUR MISSION

To continue Christ's healing ministry in bringing healing to the greatest number of people through healers with good ethical and moral standards who are motivated and united in their common respect for fundamental human values

GOALS

To strengthen and improve the hospital's ability to provide and sustain health care services for the por, neglected, vulnerable and marginalizes segments of the society. The provision of these services will seek to empower the people it serves to take ownership of their own individual and collective health needs.
